Nigeria

Nigeria is a country located on the western coast of Africa and is often referred to as the "Giant of Africa". This name comes from the diverse geography and its people. Nigeria has 36 states with Abuja as its Federal Capital Territory and Lagos being its largest city. Over 500 indigenous languages are spoken in Nigeria, including Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, Fula, Edo, Ibibio, Tiv and so on.
