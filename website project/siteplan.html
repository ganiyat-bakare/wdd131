Recipe Kingdom - Site Plan

Site Name

Recipe Kingdom

Reason for Selection: The name evokes a sense of vastness and abundance, suggesting a rich variety of recipes and culinary traditions, much like a vast kingdom is filled with diverse peoples and cultures.

Site Purpose

The Recipe Kingdom website aims to be a community hub for food lovers, allowing users to discover new recipes, share their culinary creations, rate recipes, comment on recipes, and foster connections among cooking enthusiasts. The site will provide features for recipe submission, browsing, searching different categories, and sharing tips.

Scenarios

  • What are some quick and easy recipes I can try for dinner?
  • How can I upload my own recipe with images?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #1871d8 (Used for headings background, subheadings and buttons)

Secondary Color: #ccc (Used for inputs and textarea)

Background Color: #f9f9f9 (Used for the body background)

Neutral Color: #fff (Used for headings and links)

Accent1 Color: #333 (Used for the footer backgrounds)

Accent2 Color: #e6eaee (Used for cards and highlights)

Accent3 Color: #e74c3c (Used for toggle buttons)

Accent4 Color: #c0392b (Used for toggle buttons hover)

Typography

Font 1: Lato - Used for body text.

Font 2: Tinos - Used for headings to add elegance and readability.
